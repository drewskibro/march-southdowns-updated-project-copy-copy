Beyond the Scale

What changes when you lose weight medically

The benefits of GLP-1 treatment go far beyond appearance. Patients consistently report improvements across every area of their health.

Joint Pain Relief

Every kilogram lost removes roughly 4kg of pressure from your knees. Patients report significant reductions in joint pain within weeks.

Blood Sugar Control

GLP-1 medications directly improve insulin sensitivity. Many patients with pre-diabetes see their blood sugar return to normal range.

Energy Restored

Carrying less weight and improved metabolic function means more energy for daily life — patients frequently describe feeling like a different person.

Blood Pressure Reduced

Weight loss of just 5–10% can produce meaningful reductions in blood pressure, lowering the risk of heart disease and stroke.

Healthier Food Relationship

By quieting food noise at a neurological level, GLP-1 medications help you make better choices without white-knuckling every meal.

Sustainable Long-Term Results

With monthly pharmacist support and dose optimisation, Southdowns patients maintain results far beyond what dieting alone can achieve.

Your Journey

How it works at Southdowns

From your first conversation to achieving your goal weight — here's exactly what to expect at every step.

1

Free Consultation

Book online or call any of our 4 Hampshire branches. Your pharmacist will review your BMI, health history, and weight loss goals — at no charge — to confirm you're a suitable candidate and recommend the right medication.

Takes around 20 minutes
2

Same-Day Prescription

If you're eligible, your pharmacist can issue and dispense your prescription on the same day — no waiting for a GP referral, no separate pharmacy visit. You leave with your medication and a clear injection guide.

No GP referral needed
3

Monthly Check-Ins

Each month you return to your local Southdowns branch for a face-to-face review. Your pharmacist tracks progress, adjusts your dose if needed, answers questions, and renews your prescription — keeping you safe and on track.

Face-to-face at any of our 4 branches
4

Reach Your Goal Weight

Most patients reach their target weight within 9–12 months. Once there, your pharmacist will work with you on a maintenance plan — gradually tapering medication while embedding the lifestyle habits that keep the weight off for good.
